01

Introduction

Welcome to UdharPay ("we", "our", or "us"). UdharPay is a smart credit management application designed for Indian shopkeepers to track udhar (credit), manage customers, and recover payments efficiently.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile application. By using UdharPay, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

🇮🇳 UdharPay is built for Indian shopkeepers and complies with applicable Indian data protection laws including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 (DPDP Act).

02

Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide our services:

Data Type What We Collect Why
Business Info Shop name, owner name, UPI ID, phone Personalize your experience
Customer Data Names, phone numbers, addresses Manage your customer list
Transaction Data Udhar amounts, dates, payment records Track your business finances
Photos Customer photos, bill photos (optional) Visual identification & records
Device Data Anonymous device ID, app version Crash reporting & improvements
Usage Data Features used, session duration Improve app performance
03

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and operate the UdharPay application and its features
  • Enable cloud backup and sync across your devices (only when you turn it on)
  • Process in-app purchases and premium subscriptions via Google Play
  • Send push notifications for payment reminders and app updates (only with permission)
  • Power AI features using Google Gemini (data sent is anonymized where possible)
  • Generate PDF reports and invoices for your business
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Process referral rewards

🚫 We NEVER sell, rent, or share your personal or customer data with third parties for marketing purposes.

04

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in the following ways:

  • Local Storage: All your customer and transaction data is stored on your device using encrypted local storage
  • Cloud Storage (Optional): If you enable Cloud Sync, your data is stored in Google Firebase Firestore with end-to-end encryption
  • Photos: Stored locally in your device's app directory, never uploaded unless cloud sync is enabled
  • Backups: Local backup files are stored on your device and shared only when you choose to export

🔒 Cloud data is encrypted and only accessible using your unique Firebase authentication ID. No one, including UdharPay team, can access your business data.

05

Each service has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com.

06

App Permissions

UdharPay requests the following device permissions:

  • Camera: To take customer photos and bill photos for records
  • Storage/Photos: To save and retrieve customer photos and backups
  • Internet: For AI features, cloud sync, and ads
  • Biometrics/Fingerprint: For app lock security (data never leaves device)
  • Notifications: For payment reminders and app alerts
  • Vibration: For haptic feedback on interactions

All permissions are requested only when needed and can be revoked from your device settings at any time.

07

Your Rights & Controls

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is visible within the app at all times
  • Export: Export all data as PDF, CSV, or backup file anytime
  • Delete Local Data: Settings → Danger Zone → Clear All Data
  • Delete Cloud Data: Settings → Cloud Sync → Delete Cloud Data
  • Disable Cloud Sync: Turn off in Settings → Cloud Sync anytime
  • Opt out of Ads: Upgrade to Premium to remove all advertisements
  • Revoke Permissions: Via Android Settings → Apps → UdharPay → Permissions
08

Children's Privacy

UdharPay is designed for business use by adults (18+). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

Continued use of UdharPay after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
